About

PyData Boston is the Boston-area chapter of PyData, an educational program of NumFOCUS. We host monthly events focusing on topics in open source technology, analysis and science. We seek to engage everyone in the community from all backgrounds and of all experience levels in a common conversation of what technology can do and how we can use it. We're fully volunteer-driven; our organizers, audience and speakers volunteer their time to be part of the conversation. Join us!
